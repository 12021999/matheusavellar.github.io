DTEbot Commands List

    Below is a list of all of DTE Bot's commands, followed by their descriptions.
    If you have any doubt, please look for a DTE Staff member to help you out!

  • ping

    Replies "pong!" when DTE bot is running

  • rules

    Displays the DTE room rules

  • op

    Displays the DTE OverPlayed list

  • noskip

    Disables automatically skipping the next song

  • kill

    Kills the bot

  • reload

    Reloads the bot

  • eta

    Displays your Estimated Time of Arrival

  • dc

    Moves you to your lost spot, in case you disconnect

  • meh

    Sets required amount of Meh's to skip a song

  • givespot

    Gives your spot to another user

  • help

    Links this help site
