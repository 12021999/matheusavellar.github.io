About

 

The Game

Project Avelludo was developed to present to the public what the daily life at CJLL - NAVE (later referred to only as NAVE) feels like from the student perspective. The game quite surprisingly well represents the everyday struggle to get everything done and dusted before exams, and also to learn each and every subject without forgetting any homework. Rated 10/10 by my mother, this game is an incredible experience that must not be overestimated.

Us

We, developers of Project Avelludo, are High School students, and proudly participate in the Programming course from Colégio José Leite Lopes - NAVE. We here have the opportunity to study and develop digital and analog games on their most exciting iterations.
